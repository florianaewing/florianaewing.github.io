Description

The following selection of images consists of a series of drawings rendered with acrylic pigment. My inspiration for the work collected here is motivated by an appreciation for the mundane. In my many attempts to capture some tangible slice of the real world through abstract expression, sometimes everything goes just right from start to finish. I hope you enjoy viewing this small selection of examples of what can happen on the rare occasion that I manage to express my imagination fluently.
