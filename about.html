About the Artist

Florian Ewing is a Seattle-based visual artist and student Data Scientist. The images contained in this virtual gallery were primarily produced at his workshop in View Ridge. His body of work has been hosted in several restaurants and galleries around Seattle, with the next exhibition planned for September, 2025 at Cafe Javasti Wedgwood, where he will be presenting a new series of framed original acrylic paintings. Please enjoy the gallery. To request more information or contact the artist, refer to the Contact page.
